Graham Mango Float

Filipino mango float is a no-bake dessert made with layers of graham crackers, cream, and ripe mangoes. Simple yet delicious, it requires only a handful of ingredients and is best chilled overnight so the cream sets and the crackers soften. Light, creamy, and perfectly sweet, it’s a refreshing treat ideal for hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of crushed graham
  • 20 pieces Graham crackers
  • 1 cup all purpose cream or coolwhip
  • 3/4 cup condensed milk
  • 2 cups of mangoes thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Arrange 8–10 graham crackers in a rectangular dish.

  2. Transfer the tamarind broth to a pot and bring it to a boil.

  3. In a bowl, mix cream, or cool whip, with condensed milk.

  4. Spread the mixture over the crackers, then layer thin mango slices on top.

  5. Repeat with more crackers, cream, and mangoes to create several layers.

  6. Finish with mangoes and crushed grahams on top.

  7. Chill for at least 3 hours before serving.
