Oven Roasted Asparagus Recipe

This oven-roasted asparagus recipe is as straightforward as it gets. A sprinkle of Parmesan cheese adds a subtle richness, making the flavors stand out even more with just a small touch.

Beyond its taste, asparagus is packed with vitamins A, C, and K—nutrients that support immunity, bone health, and healthy skin. With both flavor and nutrition in one dish, it’s no wonder asparagus often finds its way onto the table..

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons parmesan cheese
  • 3 heads garlic minced

How To Cook

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Place the asparagus on a baking tray and drizzle with olive oil, tossing gently to coat.

  2. Heat 3 tablespoons of cooking oil in a deep pot.

  3. Season with salt and ground black pepper. Add garlic and Parmesan cheese, then toss again until everything is evenly mixed.

  4. Bake for about 13 minutes, until the asparagus is tender and slightly crisp at the tips.

  5. Remove from the oven and transfer to a serving plate. Sprinkle with extra Parmesan if desired.

  6. Serve warm and enjoy.

Tips & Tricks

  • Trim before cooking. Cut or snap off the tough, woody ends before roasting.

  • Add lemon. A squeeze of lemon (juice or zest) brightens the flavor. Add it before roasting for a subtle citrus note, or after for a stronger, zesty kick.

  • Season simply. Stick to salt, pepper, and garlic powder, or elevate with herbs like thyme, rosemary, or basil.

  • Avoid overcooking. Roast until tender but still crisp; overcooked asparagus turns mushy and loses flavor.
