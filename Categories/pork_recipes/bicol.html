Bicol Express Recipe

Bicol Express is a creamy, spicy stew with a hint of sweetness, made flavorful by ginger and local seasonings that perfectly blend the richness of the meat with the sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. pork belly sliced into strips
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup shrimp paste or salted Krill
  • 5 cloves garlic crushed
  • 2 thumbs ginger minced
  • 5 pieces Thai chili pepper chopped
  • 1 piece onion crushed
  • 2 pieces Serrano pepper sliced
  • 1 cup water optional
  • 8 grams Maggi Magic Sarap

How To Cook

  1. In a pan, combine pork belly, ginger, garlic, onion, chili peppers, shrimp paste, and coconut milk. Mix well, cover, and bring to a boil.

  2. Lower the heat and simmer for about 50 minutes, adding water as needed.

  3. Stir in the remaining coconut cream and more shrimp paste if desired.

  4. Season with Maggi Magic Sarap and continue cooking on low until the sauce thickens.

  5. Transfer to a serving plate and serve hot with rice.
