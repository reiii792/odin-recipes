Bulalo Recipe

Filipinos are known for their love of stews and soups—whether it’s the sour tang of sinigang or the comforting sweetness of tinola, there’s a dish for every taste. Among the most popular and beloved is Bulalo, a rich beef shank stew that’s hearty, flavorful, and perfect for cool nights.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef shank
  • ½ cabbage whole leaf individually detached
  • 1 bundle bok choy
  • 2 cobs corn each cut into 3 parts
  • 2 Tablespoons whole peppercorn
  • 1/2 cup green onions cut unto 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 onion
  • 34 ounces water
  • fish sauce to taste optional

How To Cook

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil.

  2. Add beef shank, onion, and peppercorns, then simmer for about 1 ½ hours, or 30 minutes in a pressure cooker, until tender.

  3. Add corn and cook for 10 minutes.

  4. Stir in fish sauce, cabbage, pechay, and green onions. Serve hot and enjoy!
